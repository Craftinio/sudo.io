# Privacy Policy – Sudo- Classic Sudoku Game

Last updated: December 2025

Thank you for using **Sudo**, our mobile Sudoku application.  
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1. Automatically Collected Data
We use third-party services (such as Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics) that may collect:
- Device information (model, operating system, language)
- App usage data (sessions, features used)
- General analytics data
- Advertising identifiers (for personalized or non-personalized ads)

We **do not collect any personally identifiable information** (name, email, phone number, etc.)

### 1.2. AdMob
Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads.  
AdMob may use your device’s advertising ID to provide relevant ads.

For details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 2. How We Use Data
Collected data is used solely for:
- Improving app performance
- Fixing bugs
- Enhancing user experience
- Displaying ads through AdMob

We never sell or share your data with third parties except:
- Analytics providers
- Advertising partners (AdMob)

---

## 3. Children’s Privacy
Our app is not targeted toward children under 13.  
We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

---

## 4. Data Security
We use secure third-party services (Google Firebase, Google Ads) which follow industry security standards.  
We do not store any personal data ourselves.

---

## 5. User Rights
You may:
- Disable personalized ads from your device settings  
- Reset advertising ID  
- Opt out of analytics tracking via OS settings
